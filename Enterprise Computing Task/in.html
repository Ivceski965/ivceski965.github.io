How To Protect

Four easy ways to improve your protection today.

These practical steps work for most people and devices. Start with one and build better protection over time.

1. Use strong passwords

Choose unique passphrases and use a password manager to keep them secure.

2. Update regularly

Install updates for your software, apps, and devices to block known risks.

3. Watch for scams

Be careful with unexpected messages, links, or requests for personal details.

4. Protect your privacy

Review app permissions and share only the information you need.
