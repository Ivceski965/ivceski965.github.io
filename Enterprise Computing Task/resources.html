Trusted Sources

Cyber security resources worth bookmarking.

Hand-picked links to official bodies, practical tools, and learning platforms — all free, all reputable.

Official Bodies

Government & national agencies

These organisations publish advisories, incident reports, and actionable guidance backed by national-level intelligence.

UK Official

National Cyber Security Centre

The UK's authority on cyber threats. Publishes threat reports, guidance for individuals and organisations, and the Cyber Essentials certification framework.

ncsc.gov.uk →
AU Official

Australian Cyber Security Centre

Australia's lead agency for cyber security. Offers the Essential Eight framework, threat intelligence, and a 24/7 cyber hotline.

cyber.gov.au →
US Official

CISA — Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency

US federal agency that coordinates national cyber defence, maintains the Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalogue, and issues emergency directives.

cisa.gov →
EU Official

ENISA — EU Agency for Cybersecurity

The European Union's dedicated cyber security agency. Publishes the annual Threat Landscape report and policy recommendations across member states.

enisa.europa.eu →
INT Official

INTERPOL Cybercrime

International coordination on cybercrime investigations. Publishes global threat assessments and runs the Gateway platform for reporting online fraud.

interpol.int →
US Reporting

FBI — Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3)

The FBI's dedicated portal for reporting internet-enabled crime. Publishes the annual Internet Crime Report with data on losses across attack types.

ic3.gov →
Free Tools

Check, scan, and verify

Practical tools you can use right now to check files, links, passwords, and whether your data has been exposed.

Tool Free

Have I Been Pwned?

Check whether your email address or phone number appears in a known data breach. Covers 14+ billion compromised accounts across hundreds of incidents.

haveibeenpwned.com →
Tool Free

VirusTotal

Upload a file or paste a URL to scan it against 70+ antivirus engines simultaneously. Useful for checking downloads before opening them.

virustotal.com →
Tool Research

Shodan

Search engine for internet-connected devices. Security researchers use it to find exposed cameras, routers, and industrial systems open to the public internet.

shodan.io →
Tool Free

Firefox Monitor

Mozilla's breach monitoring service. Sign up to receive email alerts when your address appears in a newly discovered breach, powered by Have I Been Pwned.

monitor.firefox.com →
Tool Website

SSL Labs Server Test

Free deep analysis of the SSL/TLS configuration of any public web server. Rates encryption quality and flags certificate misconfigurations.

ssllabs.com →
Tool Network

GRC ShieldsUP!

Instantly probes your internet-facing ports to reveal what is visible and accessible from outside your network — a quick personal firewall audit.

grc.com →

Reporting helps authorities track patterns, shut down operations, and warn others. It takes less than five minutes.
